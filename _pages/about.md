I am a PhD student in the department of Computer Science at [RWTH Aachen University](https://www.rwth-aachen.de/). I am working in the [Institute of Computational Genomics](https://www.costalab.org/) of University Hospital RWTH Aachen with Prof. Dr. Ivan Costa. My current research interest is in appling and developing machine learning methods to analyze single cell RNA/ATAC-seq data.
