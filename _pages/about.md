I am a PhD student in the department of Computer Science at RWTH Aachen University. I am working in the [Institute of Computational Genomics](https://www.costalab.org/) of University Hospital RWTH Aachen with Prof. Dr. Ivan Costa. My current research interest is in appling and developing machine learning methods to analysze single cell RNA/ATAC-seq data. 

I receved the BSc and MSc degrees in computer science from the College of Computer Science and Technology, [Jilin University](http://global.jlu.edu.cn), China and the School of Computer, [Central China Normal University](http://english.ccnu.edu.cn/), China in July 2012 and June 2016, respectively. During my master's study, I specialized in the area of evolutionary computation under supervision of Dr. Jinglei Guo.
